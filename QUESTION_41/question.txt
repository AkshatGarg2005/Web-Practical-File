question: 15) Methods used to provide effects in jQuery.
answer:

Visibility: show(), hide(), toggle().

Fade: fadeIn(), fadeOut(), fadeToggle(), fadeTo(opacity).

Slide: slideDown(), slideUp(), slideToggle().

Custom: animate(props, duration, easing, complete) (numeric CSS only).

Control helpers: stop(), delay(), finish(), promise().

Params/notes: duration in ms/'fast'/'slow'; optional easing/callback; effects queue per element—don’t overuse for performance.
